About Me

Mostafa Mansour is a professor of quantum physics at Hassan II University in Casablanca, Morocco. He earned his PhD in Mathematical Physics from Mohammed V University of Rabat, with a specialization in quantum mechanics, quantum computing, and quantum information theory. His research is dedicated to advancing the theoretical underpinnings of quantum systems and investigating their applications in quantum information science and emerging quantum technologies. He is actively engaged in academic instruction and science outreach, striving to make complex quantum phenomena accessible through university lectures and public talks that foster scientific literacy and interdisciplinary engagement. Prof. Mansour collaborates with international research teams, contributes regularly to peer-reviewed journals, and supervises graduate students working on frontier topics in quantum science.
